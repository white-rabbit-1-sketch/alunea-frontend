Settings

Here, you can find your settings.

Account email

Your email address serves as the login for your Alunea account and is also used to receive important service-related communications. Please note that this email address is distinct from your mailboxes and will not receive forwarded emails from aliases.

Account password

Updating your password is an essential step in maintaining the security of your Alunea account. Please note that after changing your password, all active sessions except the current one will be terminated as a security measure.

Two-Factor Authentication coming soon

Enhance the security of your Alunea account by enabling two-factor authentication (2FA). Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of protection to your account, requiring not only a password but also a second form of verification to access your account.

Favorite domain

Choose your preferred domain below. This domain will be automatically selected by default when adding new aliases for your convenience.

Favorite mailbox

Choose your preferred mailbox below. This mailbox will be automatically selected by default when adding new aliases for your convenience.

Language

Choose Your Language. Please note that selecting a language here affects only the communications on our website, such as messages and notifications. It does not change the language of the entire application. If you need to switch the language of the entire application, you can do so from the top menu.

Account delete

We're sorry to see you go! If you no longer wish to use Alunea, you have the option to delete your account. Please be aware that deleting your account is irreversible and will permanently remove all data associated with your account, including your mailboxes, aliases, settings, and any domains.

Need help ?

Here, you'll find answers to common queries about settings. If you have a question that's not listed below, feel free to reach out to us for assistance.

What happens when I change my password?

When you change your password, your current password will be replaced with the new one. Additionally, for security reasons, all active sessions except the current one will be terminated to ensure that only you have access to your account.

What happens when I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

Enabling two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. After enabling 2FA, you will be required to enter a verification code from your chosen method in addition to your password every time you log in to your Alunea account. This helps protect your account from unauthorized access, even if your password is compromised.

What happens when I change my email address?

When you change your email address, your new email address will replace the current one associated with your Alunea account. Please note that your email address serves as your login for Alunea and is used to receive important service-related communications. However, changing your email address will not affect your mailboxes or aliases.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your Alunea account if you no longer wish to use our services. However, please note that deleting your account will permanently remove all data associated with your account, including your mailboxes, aliases, and settings. Once deleted, this action cannot be undone.

Can I mark a domain as a favorite?

Certainly. You can do it on the settings page.

What does it mean to mark a domain as favorite?

Marking a domain as favorite ensures it's automatically chosen when adding a new alias, enhancing convenience.

Can I mark a mailbox as a favorite?

Certainly. You can do it on the settings page.

What does it mean to mark a mailbox as favorite?

Marking a mailbox as favorite ensures it's automatically chosen when adding a new alias, enhancing convenience.
