FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We are Always Here to Help You

Why do I need Alunea?

In today's world, more and more services require email address, using it to gather information, send spam, sell to marketers, and for analytics purposes. Alunea protects your email from being shared with third parties without any negative effects.

What's a problem with the current email ?

You likely have an email address. You often use it to register for various services. Over time, your email address may end up in spam databases, and you start receiving unwanted emails. At some point, the amount of spam becomes so overwhelming that nothing seems to help. Alternatively, your email might be sold to marketers, who then use it to link your data, track your search queries, and so on. And not least, a leaked email address poses information security risks, especially if the same email is used across different services.

How can Alunea help?

Using the Alunea service, you can create email aliases that are linked to your real email. For example, your real mailbox is john@real.com. Instead of using it to register for various services, you can create an alias, such as alias@m.alunea.io, and use this alias when registering for services. Then the magic happens – any emails sent to alias@m.alunea.io (the alias) will automatically appear in your real mailbox john@real.com. Moreover, you can reply to any incoming emails from your real mailbox, john@real.com, and the magic happens again – the emails will be sent not from your real mailbox, but from the alias alias@m.alunea.io. This way, no one will ever know your real email address. And thanks to the ability to enable/disable aliases, you can flexibly manage your email channels.

Do you store my emails?

No, we don't store it in any form. Customer privacy is our top priority. We only forward emails and promptly delete them thereafter.

Can I use aliases for important services like banks, government websites, etc.?

Yes, you can. We don't read your emails, store them on our servers, or use them in any way.

What is an alias?

An alias in Alunea is an alternative email address that can be used instead of your primary email address. It helps you protect your privacy and manage incoming emails effectively.

How does an alias work?

When you create an alias in Alunea, any emails sent to that alias will be automatically forwarded to your real email address. This allows you to use different email addresses for different purposes while still managing all your emails in one place.

What is a mailbox?

A mailbox in Alunea is a representation of the user's actual email address. It serves as the destination for emails forwarded from aliases. Essentially, it is your primary email inbox within Alunea.

How does a mailbox work?

When you create aliases in Alunea, you can choose to forward emails sent to those aliases to your designated mailbox. Any emails sent to aliases associated with your mailbox will be automatically forwarded to your mailbox.

What is a subdomain?

A subdomain in Alunea is a custom domain extension that is exclusively owned by the user who creates it. It serves as a personalized address for creating aliases and organizing email correspondence.

How does a subdomain work?

Once created, a subdomain acts as an extension of Alunea's main domains, allowing users to create aliases on their custom subdomains. These aliases can be used to receive emails and organize communication effectively.
